How To Play
This game is like regular Black Jack. Theres the dealer hand which is the house and theres player hand which is you the player. The goal is to get a bigger hand than the dealer or get 21. There are no draws since house always wins.
There are 3 buttons: Start, Hit, and Stay. The Start button is to start the game and also clears the game and give you a new hand you want to start a new game. The hit button allows you to get another card. Lastly the stay buttons allow you to stay at where you are. After pressing stay or pressing hit and its above 21 or hits 21 the game is over and a message telling you who won will appear.
Goal of the game. The goal of the game is to either get Black Jack which is 2 cards in hand and both add up to 21. The other one is to get a higher score than the Dealer without busting or going over 21. The dealer can also bust too. If the dealer has less than 17 in their hands they will also have to keep hitting until they are there or over.
